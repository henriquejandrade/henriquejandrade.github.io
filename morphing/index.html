Morphing Recife

Here are some pieces of video using Beier & Neely's morphing technique. The videos show the evolution of Recife, Brazil's landscape. They'll together compose a larger video with the history of the city. That's some work in progress!

You can read the article describing the technique here.

Avenida Guararapes (1940 to 2017)

Downtown's urban transformation with the rise of tall buildings and a late abandonment of the area. Boa Vista area.

Brasília Teimosa

The video shows the advance of the city growth over the sea, following a series of landfilling irregularly occupied and left as is. It comprehends Pina, Brasilia Teimosa, Cabanga and Sao Jose neighborhoods.

Cemitério de Santo Amaro (from 1848)

Santo Amaro area, showing its cemmetery and 13 de Maio's city park.

Boa Vista (from 1952)

Video showing the height growth of the central area of Recife.
